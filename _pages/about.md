I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the [Department of Economics](https://www.econ.berkeley.edu/) at UC Berkeley, with research fields in public and labor economics. The general focus of my research is how the US tax, transfer, and social insurance system shapes worker-firm labor market dynamics. More recently, my work examines the extent to which workers understand and make use of public policies to reduce income volatility.

If you're also interested in these ideas or would like to chat, please [get in touch!](https://sreekancherla.github.io/contact/)
